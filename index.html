Why Math for Code?

• Numbers and bases power parsing, serialization, hashing, bitwise ops, and performance work.
• Logs appear in complexity analysis, data scales, entropy, information theory, and ML preprocessing.
• Polynomials model curves, cost, and approximations; roots help in geometry, physics, and algorithms.
• Primes underpin cryptography, modular arithmetic, and randomized algorithms.
